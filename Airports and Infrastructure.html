Airports and Infrastructure

1.Runways:

Runways are the main strips of pavement where aircraft take off and land. They are designed to handle different aircraft types and weather conditions.

2.Taxiways:

Taxiways connect runways with terminals, hangars, and other airport facilities. They guide aircraft between these points safely on the ground.

3.Terminals:

Terminals are buildings where passengers check in, go through security, board aircraft, and collect baggage after landing.

4.Air Traffic Control (ATC):

ATC is responsible for directing aircraft both on the ground and in the air to ensure safety and efficiency in flight operations.

5.Ground Services and Logistics:

These services include fueling, baggage handling, catering, maintenance, and cargo operations necessary for running airport activities smoothly.
